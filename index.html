    A Message from the Coder

    jjdemtiriuk @ relutionDev - 15/02/2015

    Welcome to foundationUltra here is our demo to show off some of the elements that foundationUltra has to offer from not only foundationApps such as the new flexbox grid but also foundationSites and angular-foundation aswell as increasing the range of icons by using font-awesome

    If you are viewing this on a desktop then you should see 3 columns of data, a sidebar the messages and then this panel. If you make the screen smaller or view this on your phone or tablet then you will see the magic happen, the topbar will turn into its famous mobile format aswell as the new elements such as the side bar turning in offcanvas panel that slide on. Go on give it a try
